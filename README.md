## 🎧 Overview

My Sound Diary is a running record of the songs that have soundtracked my days — not a curated "best of" playlist, but a raw log of what I'm actually listening to, when, and why.

No algorithm, no recommendation engine — just tracks added by hand, sorted by mood, genre, or whatever phase I'm going through at the time.

---

## ✨ Features

* Daily / weekly listening log
* Sorted by mood, genre, or season
* Notes on why a track made the cut
* Simple Markdown format, easy to browse
* Updated whenever something new sticks

---

## 🎯 Project Goals

* Keep a personal archive of musical taste over time
* Make it easy to look back and remember a phase through its soundtrack
* Discover patterns in mood vs. genre over the months
* Share recommendations with friends, casually
